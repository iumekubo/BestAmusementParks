Here are some great roller coasters at each park!

Lets begin with Disneyland, Incredicoster is a wonderful coaster. It is a launch coaster and has one loop, with the theme of the movie Incredibles 2. Next their is Legoland with the Dragon. It is a classic coaster, with a Lego dragon theme. At Universal Studios, there is Harry Potter and the Forbidden Journey which is a part simulation, part coaster. It is a Harry Potter themed attraction with 4-D like experiences. Tatsu at six flags is a wonderful thrill ride. This dragon themed ride is a hanging coaster that flips you on your belly so it feels like you are flying. Knott's Berry farm has a classic wooden coaster called ghost rider, that is very fast and thrilling.
